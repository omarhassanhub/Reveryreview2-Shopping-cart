FAQ

How do I search for a product?

You can browse or search for the books you would like to order using our main categories, Fiction, Non-fiction etc. You can also use the Search function at the top right of each page for a quicker search, by author name.

How do I place an order?

Select your item(s) and click on the Add to cart button. If you are ready to checkout, click on the Basket tab. You will be directed to an easy to use step-by-step journey to purchase your books.

Can I deliver to a different address than my home address?

Yes, you can choose a different address to deliver your books to other than your billing address by clicking on the option on the Delivery Information page. You can also edit your details during the checkout process.
